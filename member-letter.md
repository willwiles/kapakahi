## We've Been Building Something for You

Over the past few months, we've been working behind the scenes on something we're genuinely excited about — a brand-new online experience at **mokudetail.com** that was designed with one person in mind: you.

Here's what's waiting for you:

**Your Own Member Account.** You now have a personal dashboard where you can manage everything in one place. View your membership details, track your booking history, see your loyalty rewards, and schedule your next clean — all from your phone, anytime, anywhere. No more texting back and forth to find a time. No more wondering when your last detail was. It's all right there.

**Online Booking That Actually Works.** We built our booking system from scratch to make scheduling as effortless as possible. Pick your service, choose your date and time, upload photos of your vehicle so we come prepared, and you're done. If you're already a member, the system recognizes you — just enter your email, log in, and your details auto-fill. It takes less than a minute.

**Monthly Clean Credits.** This is the part we're most excited about. As a member, your plan now comes with monthly clean credits that you can use to book directly through the website — no payment needed at checkout. Your credits refresh every month, and you can see exactly how many you have left right from your dashboard. It's the easiest way to keep your vehicle looking fresh without ever thinking about it.

**Loyalty Rewards.** We've always believed that the more you ride with us, the more we should give back. Your account now tracks every visit and automatically unlocks rewards as you level up — from the Reef Rookie tier all the way to Kai Legend status. The perks get better the longer you stay.

**Appointment Reminders.** You'll receive a confirmation email the moment you book, plus an SMS reminder the day before your appointment. We want to make sure you never miss a detail — and that we never miss a chance to make your day.
