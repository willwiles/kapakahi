Meet Mandy

Mandy Kēhau Sinclair — Owner

Pono Home Co. was founded with a simple mission: give O'ahu families and vacation rental hosts their time back. Mandy Kēhau Sinclair brings the warmth of Hawaiian hospitality to every clean — detail-oriented, reliable, and deeply invested in the wellbeing of every client.

Whether you run a short-term rental and need a flawless guest-ready turnover, or you're a busy family craving a truly spotless home, Mandy's team handles it all — cleaning, organizing, decluttering, and everything in between.

Services We Provide

Residential Cleaning

Comprehensive top-to-bottom home cleaning. Kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, common areas — every corner done right so you can come home to clean.

STR & Vacation Rental Turnover

Fast, thorough, and guest-ready. We reset your Airbnb, VRBO, or short-term rental between stays — linens, restocking, and a 5-star finish.

Regular Maintenance Cleans
